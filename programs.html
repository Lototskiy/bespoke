Genetic Testing

Cancer

A service composed of general and gender-specific cancers. It is recommended for adults with a family history of cancer or for those who want to improve their quality of life through proactive prevention against cancer.

Gastric cancer, lung cancer, colorectal cancer, hepatocellular carcinoma, thyroid cancer, renal cell carcinoma, pancreatic cancer.

Male cancers: prostate cancer, testicular cancer, bladder cancer, esophageal cancer.

Female cancers: breast cancer, cervical cancer, endometrial cancer, ovarian cancer.

Common Disease

A service composed of common diseases, excluding cancer. It is recommended for adults with a family history of common diseases or those who think that they could be especially vulnerable to these diseases.

Chronic Hepatitis, non-alcoholic fatty liver disease, Crohn’s disease, ulcerative colitis, type 2 diabetes mellitus, obesity, hyperlipidemia, systemic lupus erythematosus, psoriasis, atopic dermatitis, periodontal disease, allergic rhinitis, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, asthma, glaucoma, macular degeneration, gout, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, osteoporosis, stroke, dementia, chronic artery disease, atrial fibrillation, myocardial infarction, high blood pressure, brain aneurysm, (+Female: polycystic ovary syndrome).

Platinum

A comprehensive health care service that provides information on drug reactions and physical traits such as alcohol and nicotine dependence, in addition to cancer and common diseases.

Cancer 11 Types + Common Disease 27 Types + Physical Characteristics 6 Types + Food/Drug Response 4 Types.

Physical Characteristics: fibrinogen response to ambient particulate material, malocclusion, triglycerides level, alcohol dependence, nicotine dependence, high myopia.

Food/Drug Response: warfarin maintenance dose, cyclophosphamide- induced adverse drug reactions, ACEI therapy in hypertensive, methamphetamine psychosis.

Platinum Lite

A solid configuration that allows you to see the onset of cancer and common diseases at once. Itvis recommended for those who have a family history of cancer or common diseases or those who want to improve the quality of life through proactive prevention against disease.

Cancer 11 Types + Common Disease 27 Types.

Noble

A one stop solution that covers all the services, ranging from cancers to common and rare diseases.

Cancer 11 Types + Common Disease 27 Types + Physical Characteristics 6 Types + Food/Drug Response 4 Types + Rare Disease 44 Types.

Metabolic Syndrome Disease

It is a composition that can detect the possibility of developing metabolic syndrome related diseases. It is recommended for people who have a family history related to metabolic syndrome or think that management is needed for metabolic syndrome.

Myocardial Infarction, non-alcoholic fatty liver disease, high blood pressure, stroke, type 2 diabetes mellitus, obesity, hyperlipidemia, coronary artery disease.

Geriatric Disease

It is recommended for those who want to prepare for a healthy life in their advanced years through services composed of geriatric diseases that are likely to occur in later years.

Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, periodontal disease, glaucoma, macular degeneration, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, osteoporosis, stroke, dementia.

Kids

It is a service consisting of chronic diseases that occur frequently in childhood. People who want to check the risk of genetic diseases such as atopy, asthma, and rhinitis regardless of age can also be tested.

Asthma, atopic dermatitis, allergic rhinitis, obesity, type 1 diabetes mellitus, type 2 diabetes mellitus, high myopia, dental deformities, reaction to find dust.
